About James

James Smith is the Senior Editor and lead precious metals analyst at GoldPriceTools. With over 15 years of experience in the bullion and scrap metal markets across the UK and Europe, James provides clear, accurate, and actionable insights into the complexities of gold and silver pricing.

Before joining GoldPriceTools, James worked extensively with independent jewellers and regional bullion dealers, developing a deep understanding of hallmarking standards, spot price mechanics, and fair-value assessment for scrap metals. He is passionate about market transparency and helping consumers understand exactly what their precious metals are worth.

At GoldPriceTools, James oversees the editorial direction of our guides, ensuring all content is factually accurate, up-to-date with current LBMA standards, and easy to understand for both industry professionals and everyday consumers.
