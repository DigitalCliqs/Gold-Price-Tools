Authors & Editorial Team

GoldPriceTools content is produced and reviewed by an in-house editorial team rather than published under personal bylines. We use this organisational byline model to keep our editorial process transparent while protecting the privacy of individual contributors in line with UK GDPR.

Our editorial bylines

GoldPriceTools Editorial Team

The editorial group responsible for researching, drafting, fact-checking and publishing every page on this site. All spot-price data is sourced from the London Bullion Market Association via gold-api.com.

How we work

Every published page is reviewed against primary sources before going live: LBMA reference prices for spot, HMRC for UK tax guidance, the Royal Mint for British coin specifications, and the World Gold Council for market context. Live spot prices auto-refresh from gold-api.com; the dateModified on each page reflects the date of its most recent human editorial review, not the price refresh time.

For full disclosure of our sources, AI-assistance policy, corrections process and contact route, see our Editorial Standards page.
