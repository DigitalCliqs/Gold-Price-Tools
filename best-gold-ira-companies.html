What Is a Gold IRA?

A gold Individual Retirement Account (IRA) is a specialised, self-directed IRA that allows investors to hold physical precious metals as qualified retirement investments. Unlike traditional IRAs that typically hold paper assets like stocks, bonds, and mutual funds, a gold IRA acts as a tangible hedge against inflation and economic volatility.

To qualify for a gold IRA, the IRS mandates strict purity requirements: gold must be 99.5% pure, and silver must be 99.9% pure. Additionally, the metals cannot be stored at home; they must be held in an IRS-approved depository by a qualified custodian.

How to Open a Gold IRA

  1. Choose a Reputable Gold IRA Company: Select a company from our list above based on your minimum investment capacity and service preferences.
  2. Open Your Self-Directed IRA: Your chosen company will help you fill out the paperwork to establish a new self-directed IRA with an approved custodian (e.g., Equity Trust Company).
  3. Fund the Account: You can fund your new IRA via a direct transfer or rollover from an existing retirement account (like a 401(k), 403(b), or traditional IRA) without incurring tax penalties.
  4. Purchase Precious Metals: Work with your account representative to select IRS-approved gold and silver products.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a gold IRA?

A gold IRA is a self-directed individual retirement account that allows you to invest in physical gold, silver, platinum, and palladium, rather than traditional paper assets like stocks and bonds.

How much does it cost to start a gold IRA?

Minimum investments vary by company, ranging from $2,000 (Noble Gold) to $50,000 (Augusta Precious Metals). Expect to pay around $175-$225 in annual fees for administration and storage.

Are gold IRAs safe?

Yes, gold IRAs are safe. The IRS mandates that the physical metals are stored in an approved depository, meaning your assets are secured and insured.

Can I store my gold IRA at home?

No, the IRS requires that gold and silver held in a self-directed IRA be stored in an approved depository. Home storage can result in significant tax penalties.

How do I roll over a 401(k) to a gold IRA?

To roll over a 401(k) to a gold IRA, you must open a self-directed IRA with an approved custodian, fund the account via a direct or indirect rollover, and then select the precious metals you wish to purchase.
