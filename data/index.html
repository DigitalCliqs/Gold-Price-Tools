Live Gold & Silver Price Data Feed

GoldPriceTools provides a comprehensive dataset of live spot prices for gold and silver bullion. Our real-time data feed covers multiple karats and units, including grams, troy ounces, and kilograms, giving you accurate and up-to-the-minute precious metal valuations.

We source our spot price data directly from authenticated LBMA-aligned market feeds via our API, ensuring the highest level of accuracy and reliability. To reflect the dynamic nature of the commodities market, our dataset is automatically updated every 60 seconds during active trading hours.

This dataset is open and free to use under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC-BY 4.0) licence. You are welcome to use, share, and adapt the data, provided you give appropriate credit to GoldPriceTools and include a direct link back to https://goldpricetools.com/.
