Frequently Asked Questions About Gold & Silver Prices

How much is 14K gold per gram today?

14 karat gold is 58.3% pure gold. Today's 14K gold price per gram is shown live in our calculator above and in the 14K table. The price changes continuously as the gold spot price moves on global commodity markets.

What is 10K gold worth per gram?

10 karat gold contains 41.7% pure gold (10 parts gold out of 24). The value per gram is calculated by multiplying the 24K per-gram price by 0.417. See our live 10K table for today's exact price per gram, troy oz, and kilo.

How many grams is 1 troy oz of gold?

One troy ounce equals exactly 31.1035 grams. This differs from an avoirdupois ounce (28.35g) used for everyday weight. All gold and silver spot prices are quoted in troy ounces on global markets.

What is 18K gold price per gram?

18 karat gold is 75% pure gold, making it the most popular karat for high-quality jewellery. Today's 18K per-gram price is displayed live above. 18K commands a higher price per gram than 14K or 10K due to higher purity.

How much is a gold bar worth?

A standard Good Delivery 400 troy oz gold bar is worth over $1 million at current prices. Smaller bars range from a 1 gram bar (~$100+) to a 1 kg bar (~$100,000+). Use our Gold Bar calculator tab for live bar values.

What is 925 sterling silver price today?

Sterling silver (925) contains 92.5% pure silver. Its per-gram price is today's silver spot price × 0.925 ÷ 31.1035. Use our Silver Calculator above for exact 925 sterling values in grams, ounces, and kilos.

What is the current spot price of gold and silver?

Gold and silver spot prices update every 60 seconds on this page, sourced from global commodity markets. The spot price is the current market price for immediate delivery of one troy ounce of 24K gold or .999 fine silver.

How much is one pound of gold worth?

One avoirdupois pound of gold (453.6g) is worth roughly 14–15× the troy oz spot price. One troy pound (12 troy oz) is worth 12× the spot price. Use our calculator: set weight to 1, unit to Pounds for an exact live figure.

Gold & Silver Price History

Gold Price Outlook 2025–2026

Gold has been supported by central bank buying, geopolitical uncertainty, and inflation hedging demand. Analysts' gold price predictions for 2025–2026 generally range from $2,800 to $3,500 per troy oz depending on Fed policy and USD strength.

Gold Eagle Coin Value

The 1 oz American Gold Eagle contains 91.67% gold (22K) with a total weight of 1.0909 ozt. Its melt value tracks the gold spot price × 1.0909 × 0.9167. The coin commands a premium above melt for collector and legal tender value.

Gold Price Graph — 10 Years

The gold price chart over 10 years shows significant appreciation from ~$1,200/oz in 2019 to all-time highs above $3,000 in 2025. Key drivers include quantitative easing, COVID-19 uncertainty, and central bank gold reserve expansion.
