Gold & Silver Price Guide — Frequently Asked Questions

How much is 14K gold per gram today?

14 karat gold contains 58.33% pure gold. To find the 14K price per gram, multiply the live spot price (per troy oz) by 0.5833 ÷ 31.1035. Today's live 14K price per gram is shown above in the calculator and Quick Reference panel.

What is 10K gold worth per gram?

10 karat gold is 41.67% pure gold. It is the minimum karat legal to sell as gold jewellery in the USA. Use the calculator above or see the 10K table — the price updates every 60 seconds from live spot data.

18K gold price per gram

18K gold contains 75% pure gold — the most common standard for fine jewellery in Europe. The 18K gram price = spot price × 0.750 ÷ 31.1035. See the live table above for 1g, 5g, 10g and 1 oz values.

How many grams is 1 troy oz of gold?

One troy ounce equals exactly 31.1034768 grams. This is different from an avoirdupois ounce (28.3495g) used for everyday weight. All gold and silver spot prices are quoted in troy ounces.

925 sterling silver price today

925 sterling silver is 92.5% pure silver. Multiply the live silver spot price by 0.925 ÷ 31.1035 to get the per-gram value. Use the silver calculator above for instant calculations in USD, GBP, EUR or INR.

How much is a gold bar worth?

A standard Good Delivery 400 troy oz gold bar is worth approximately $1.5–2 million at current prices. Smaller 1 oz gold bars and 1g gold bars are popular with retail investors. See the Gold Bars tab for live prices on all sizes.

Silver price per kilogram

Silver is quoted per troy oz, but the per-kilogram price = spot × 32.1507 (troy oz per kg). At recent prices around $30/oz, one kilogram of fine silver (.999) is worth approximately $960–$1,000. Use the silver calculator for live kg pricing.

Value of US silver dollars

Pre-1965 US silver dollars contain 0.7734 troy oz of 90% silver. At current silver spot prices the silver melt value is typically $20–$28+ per coin. The silver calculator tab covers .900 coin silver purity.
