Understanding Gold & Silver Prices

14K Gold Price Per Gram Today

14 karat gold is 58.33% pure gold (14 parts out of 24). Today's 14K gold price per gram = spot price ÷ 31.1035 × 0.5833. Use our live calculator for the exact current value. 14K is the most common gold karat used in US jewellery.

10K Gold Price Per Gram

10 karat gold is 41.67% pure gold — the minimum legal gold content in the US. The 10K gold price per gram = spot price ÷ 31.1035 × 0.4167. 10K gold is durable and affordable, popular for everyday jewellery.

18K Gold Price Per Gram

18 karat gold contains 75% pure gold. It is valued at spot price ÷ 31.1035 × 0.75 per gram. Preferred in European and luxury jewellery for its rich colour and high purity while maintaining wearability.

Scrap Gold Calculator

The scrap gold melt value formula is: Weight (g) × Purity × (Spot Price ÷ 31.1035). Dealers typically offer 70–90% of melt value. Our scrap gold calculator handles all karats from 8K to 24K with live spot prices.

925 Sterling Silver Price Today

.925 sterling silver is 92.5% pure silver. The sterling silver price per gram = silver spot ÷ 31.1035 × 0.925. Our silver calculator updates every 60 seconds. The kilo silver price = silver spot × 32.1507.

How Many Grams in 1 Troy Ounce?

One troy ounce = exactly 31.1035 grams. This differs from an avoirdupois ounce (28.3495 g). All precious metals spot prices — gold, silver, platinum — are quoted per troy ounce globally.

How Much is a Gold Bar Worth?

A 1 oz gold bar = current gold spot price. A 1 kg gold bar = spot × 32.1507. The famous 400 troy oz Good Delivery bar = spot × 400 — worth over $1.2 million at current prices. Use our Gold Bars tab for live values.

Silver Price Per Kilogram

Silver kilo price = silver spot price (per troy oz) × 32.1507. At $30/oz silver, the kilo price ≈ $964.52. Bulk silver investors, jewellers, and industrial buyers often price silver by the kilogram or tonne.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 14K gold price per gram today?
14 karat (14K) gold is 58.33% pure gold (14 parts out of 24). The live 14K gold price per gram is displayed at the top of this page and in our karat table. The formula is: 24K spot price per gram × 0.5833. For example, if gold is $3,000/oz, the 24K price per gram is $96.45, and 14K is $96.45 × 0.5833 = $56.25/gram.
How do I calculate 10K gold price per gram?
10 karat gold is 41.67% pure (10 ÷ 24 = 0.4167). Formula: (Gold spot price ÷ 31.1035) × 0.4167. Our calculator does this live. 10K gold is the minimum hallmark standard in the USA. At $3,000/oz spot, 10K gold per gram ≈ $40.22.
What is 18K gold price per gram?
18K gold is 75% pure (18 ÷ 24). Formula: (Spot price ÷ 31.1035) × 0.75. At $3,000/oz spot, the 18K gold price per gram ≈ $72.34. 18K is popular in high-end jewellery across Europe and Asia for its warm colour and durability.
How many grams is 1 oz of gold?
One troy ounce of gold equals exactly 31.1035 grams. Gold is always priced in troy ounces, not standard ounces. A standard (avoirdupois) ounce is only 28.3495 grams — always use troy when calculating precious metal values.
What is the current spot price of gold and silver?
The live gold and silver spot price is shown at the top of this page, updated every 60 seconds. Spot price is the benchmark real-time price for one troy ounce of pure (24K/999) metal for immediate delivery. It fluctuates continuously during market hours.
How do I use the scrap gold calculator?
Click the Scrap Gold tab. Enter the weight in grams and select the karat for each piece. Add multiple items with different karats using "+ Add item". The calculator uses the live gold spot price to show total melt value, pure gold ounces, and average purity. Dealers typically pay 70–90% of melt value.
What is 925 sterling silver worth today?
.925 sterling silver is 92.5% pure silver. Its value per gram = silver spot price ÷ 31.1035 × 0.925. The live 925 sterling silver price per gram is shown in our Quick Reference section above. Sterling silver is the standard for silverware, jewellery, and hallmarked silver items.
What is the silver price per kilogram?
The silver kilo price = silver spot price (per troy oz) × 32.1507 troy ounces per kilogram. For example, if silver is at $32/oz, the kilo price = $32 × 32.1507 ≈ $1,028.82/kg. Industrial buyers and large investors often purchase silver by the kilogram or tonne.
How much is a gold bar worth?
Gold bar values depend on size. A 1 gram gold bar ≈ spot price ÷ 31.1035. A 1 oz gold bar ≈ current spot price (~$3,000+). A 1 kg gold bar = spot × 32.1507. The iconic 400 troy oz Good Delivery bar ≈ spot × 400 (over $1.2M at current prices). Use our Gold Bars calculator tab for live values.
What is the gold price outlook and prediction?
Gold prices are driven by inflation, US dollar strength, central bank buying, and geopolitical risk. Gold hit record highs above $3,000/oz in 2025–2026, driven by central bank demand and inflation hedging. Analysts' price targets range widely; past performance does not predict future prices.
