## Executive Summary

The precious metals keyword space is dominated by high-authority incumbents (Kitco DA 74, GoldPricez DA 55, BullionByPost DA 62, GoldPrice.org DA 68). However, **25–35 keywords are genuinely winnable within 3–6 months** for a new, well-optimised static site — particularly long-tail karat/weight calculator queries and question-format searches where dedicated landing pages are sparse.

The #1 lever is **page expansion**: building 10–15 targeted landing pages (one per karat × topic cluster) to capture the long tail. Currently, every competitor ranks for thousands of keyword variants because they have hundreds of dedicated pages. One page cannot win on 85 keywords.

### Still Needed on index.html ⚠️

2. **`<time datetime="">` tags** — wrap last-updated timestamp in proper `<time>` element with ISO datetime attribute
3. **Inline karat anchor sections** — add `id="14k-gold-price"`, `id="10k-gold-price"`, `id="18k-gold-price"` to table rows so Google can deep-link

6. **Backlink acquisition** — zero authority without at least 10–20 quality links; target personal finance blogs, scrap gold forums, and Reddit r/Gold, r/Silverbugs

## AdSense Optimisation Notes

- The high CPC keywords (14K gold per gram: ~$4–8 CPC, scrap gold calculator: ~$3–6 CPC, silver price: ~$2–4 CPC) justify AdSense — these are among the highest finance CPCs available to a content site
- Place ads **between calculator result and FAQ** (highest viewability)
- Avoid too many ads above the fold (Google's Better Ads Standards)
- Once approved, test Auto Ads vs manual placement — Auto Ads often outperform on calculator/tool pages
- Target RPM of $8–15 once established (finance niche average)
