Silver coins are the most accessible way into precious metals — but a few quirks
catch beginners out. Here's what to know before you buy.

## Junk silver vs bullion coins

Pre-decimal "junk" silver coins carry no collector premium and trade close to
melt value, making them a cheap way to accumulate. Modern bullion coins like the
Britannia and Maple Leaf cost a little more but are instantly recognisable and
easy to sell.

## Mind the VAT

Unlike investment gold, **silver attracts VAT** in the UK, which raises the
effective premium. CGT-exempt British coins can offset some of that on the way out.

## Bottom line

Start with recognised coins, factor in VAT, and treat silver as the volatile,
higher-upside cousin of gold.
