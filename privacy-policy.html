Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026  |  Effective date: 23 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PureMetals, operating at goldpricetools.com ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and applicable EU GDPR standards.

1. Who We Are

PureMetals is a free online tool providing live gold and silver price calculators, karat price tables, scrap metal calculators, and precious metals charts. The site is accessible at goldpricetools.com. For privacy enquiries, you may contact us via the About page.

2. What Data We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or submit any personal information to use this site. However, the following data may be collected automatically:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and approximate geographic location (country/region level) collected via Google Analytics 4 (GA4).
  • Device data: Screen resolution, device type (desktop/mobile/tablet), and browser language preferences.
  • Cookies: First-party and third-party cookies set by Google Analytics and Google AdSense (see Section 5 below).
  • IP addresses: Your IP address may be processed by Google Analytics and Google AdSense. We have enabled IP anonymisation in GA4.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, postal address, or any financial information. No user accounts exist on this site.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data collected through this website is used for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use the site, which calculators are most used, and how to improve the user experience (via Google Analytics 4).
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Performance: To monitor site performance and diagnose technical issues.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable law and protect the rights of the site operator.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (UK/EU GDPR)

We process data on the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR): Analytics to improve our free service.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) UK GDPR): For personalised advertising cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or using the opt-out mechanisms described in Section 6.

5. Cookies

This site uses the following categories of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the site to function. These cannot be disabled. No personal data is stored in these cookies.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 uses cookies including _ga, _ga_[ID] to distinguish users and sessions. Data is retained for 14 months. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalised or non-personalised ads. These may include IDE, DSID, and other DoubleClick cookies. See Google's Advertising Policies.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking some cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Opting Out of Google Analytics and Advertising

  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent GA4 tracking.
  • Opt out of personalised Google ads at adssettings.google.com.
  • Use the NAI opt-out tool for network-level advertising opt-out.
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings (note: not all services honour this signal).

7. Third-Party Services

This site integrates with the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • GoldAPI.io — provides live gold and silver spot prices. No personal data is sent to GoldAPI.io. See GoldAPI Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics 4 — web analytics. Data transferred to Google LLC (USA) under Standard Contractual Clauses. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — advertising network operated by Google LLC. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts loaded from Google's CDN (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may log font requests. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Chart.js (jsDelivr CDN) — open-source charting library. No personal data is processed. See jsDelivr Privacy Policy.

8. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default. Advertising cookie data retention is governed by Google's policies. You can request deletion of your Google Analytics data via Google's opt-out tools.

9. Your Rights Under UK/EU GDPR

If you are located in the UK or European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of data held about you.
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for cookie-based processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the About page. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

10. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions, requests, or complaints, please visit our About page for contact details. We aim to respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.
