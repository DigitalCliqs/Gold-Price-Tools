What is 18K Gold?

18K gold contains 18 parts gold out of 24 — a purity of 75%. It is the standard for fine jewellery, high-end Swiss watches, and luxury accessories worldwide. The hallmark for 18K gold in the UK and Europe is 750. In the US you will see 18K or 18KT stamped on items.

How Is 18K Gold Price Calculated?

The 18K price per gram = 24K spot price per troy oz ÷ 31.1035 × 0.75. The LBMA spot price is the global benchmark, set twice daily in London. Our calculator fetches this live and converts to GBP using the real-time exchange rate.
