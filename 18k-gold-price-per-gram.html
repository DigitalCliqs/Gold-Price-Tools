What is 18K Gold?

18K gold contains 18 parts gold out of 24 — a purity of 75%. It is the standard for fine jewellery, high-end Swiss watches, and luxury accessories worldwide. The hallmark for 18K gold in the UK and Europe is 750. In the US you will see 18K or 18KT stamped on items.

What is 18k gold?

18K gold is a premium gold alloy consisting of 18 parts pure gold and 6 parts other metals. It has a purity of 75.0%, making it the global standard for high-end fine jewellery, engagement rings, and luxury watches.

What hallmark is 18K gold?

18K gold is typically hallmarked with 750, indicating that it consists of 750 parts per thousand (or 75.0%) pure gold. In the US, it may also be stamped as 18K or 18KT. Both marks guarantee the same gold content.
